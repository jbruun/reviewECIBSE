A total of 32 test objects were used. There were four classes of objects: animals (e.g., klipspringer, bearded dragon, vulture, etc.), plants (e.g., succulent plant, venus fly trap, cactus, etc.), immobile artifacts (e.g., hand_operated egg beater, antique tools, wooden garlic press, etc.), and mobile artifacts (e.g., mechanized toys, Newtons cradle, etc.; see Figure 1). A total of eight objects per class were employed. One object per class was used in the warm_up phase of the study, four objects per class were used in Phase 1 of the study, and three objects per class were used in both Phases 2 and 3. Test objects consisted of 4_s real_time video clips that were displayed dynamically on a laptop computer to the child.
