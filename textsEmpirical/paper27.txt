Through the introduction of water as a science theme, teachers were engaged in exploring water properties using variety of household tools (turkey_baster, clear tubes, plastic connectors, funnels and more). The water theme provided both science and culturally relevant activities that fit within each classroom setting. For example, ice fishing, which many on the reservation engage in every winter, provided an excellent opportunity to talk about the different states of water, sink float, and water flow as spring approached.
